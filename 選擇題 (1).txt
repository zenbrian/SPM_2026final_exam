### **Q1**

* **題目：** Which operating environment in the Cynefin Framework is characterized by clear *cause-and-effect relationships* and the use of **best practices**?
* **選項：** * A. Complex
* B. Chaotic
* C. Clear
* D. Complicated


* **解答：** C. Clear

### **Q2**

* **題目：** A company facing a brand-new market with unpredictable customer reactions should primarily use which approach?
* **選項：** * A. Apply standard operating procedures
* B. Rely on expert analysis only
* C. Conduct experiments and learn from outcomes
* D. Delay decisions until full information is available


* **解答：** C. Conduct experiments and learn from outcomes

### **Q3**

* **題目：** Which domain is most appropriate for situations such as **crisis management during a natural disaster**?
* **選項：** * A. Clear
* B. Complicated
* C. Complex
* D. Chaotic


* **解答：** D. Chaotic

### **Q4**

* **題目：** Which domain encourages **small-scale experimentation** to identify emerging patterns?
* **選項：** * A. Clear
* B. Complicated
* C. Complex
* D. Chaotic


* **解答：** C. Complex

### **Q5**

* **題目：** Applying rigid best practices to a highly uncertain and innovative problem is most likely to result in:
* **選項：** * A. Increased efficiency
* B. Better employee morale
* C. Poor outcomes due to misdiagnosis of the context
* D. Faster decision-making


* **解答：** C. Poor outcomes due to misdiagnosis of the context

### **Q6**

* **題目：** Which lifecycle MOST clearly assumes that **each phase must be completed before the next begins**, with minimal overlap?
* **選項：** * A. Incremental
* B. Agile
* C. Iterative
* D. Predictive


* **解答：** D. Predictive

### **Q7**

* **題目：** Which project lifecycle explicitly combines **frequent feedback** and **early delivery of usable outputs**?
* **選項：** * A. Predictive
* B. Iterative
* C. Incremental
* D. Agile


* **解答：** D. Agile

### **Q8**

* **題目：** Which lifecycle would be the **LEAST appropriate** when requirements are fixed and regulatory compliance demands detailed upfront planning?
* **選項：** * A. Predictive
* B. Iterative
* C. Incremental
* D. Agile


* **解答：** D. Agile

### **Q9**

* **題目：** A radar chart shows strong scores for Culture and Team, but low scores for Change Rate and Incremental Delivery. What is the MOST reasonable conclusion?
* **選項：** * A. Agile should still be fully adopted
* B. Predictive lifecycle is automatically superior
* C. Agile may offer limited benefits given project constraints
* D. Team capability alone compensates for project stability


* **解答：** C. Agile may offer limited benefits given project constraints

### **Q10**

* **題目：** Two projects have equally skilled teams. Project A has frequent requirement changes; Project B has fixed scope and high regulatory verification. Which statement BEST compares Agile suitability?
* **選項：** * A. Both projects are equally suitable for Agile
* B. Project B is more suitable due to stability
* C. Project A is more suitable due to adaptability needs
* D. Suitability depends only on organizational culture


* **解答：** C. Project A is more suitable due to adaptability needs
